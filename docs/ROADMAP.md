# Product Roadmap

## Milestone 1: Platform Foundation (Completed ✅)
- System Architecture Setup (NestJS + MongoDB)
- B2B Authentication & RBAC (Recruiters, Admins)
- Multi-tenancy (Workspaces / Organizations)
- Audit Logging
- Email Notifications Infrastructure

## Milestone 2: Talent Management (Completed ✅)
- B2C Candidate Authentication (JWT)
- Document Management (S3 Abstraction, Polymorphic Resumes)
- Master Data Engine (Canonical Skills, Companies, Titles)
- Career Profile Engine (Experience, Education, Projects)
- Candidate Dashboard Aggregation
- Recruiter Preview & Professional Summary Generation

## Milestone 3: Hiring Pipeline (Upcoming ⏳)
- Job Requisitions & Postings
- Application Tracking
- Kanban Board Pipelines (Sourced -> Applied -> Interview -> Offer)
- Activity Feeds & Notes

## Milestone 4: AI & Automation
- AI Resume Parsing (Extraction to Structured Profile)
- Candidate Ranking & Semantic Scoring
- Automated Email Sequences
- Smart Talent Search (Elastic/Vector)

## Milestone 5: Collaboration & Analytics
- Interview Scheduling (Calendar Integrations)
- Assessment Integrations
- Hiring Velocity & Pipeline Analytics Dashboards
- Offer Letter Generation & e-Signatures
